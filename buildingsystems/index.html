BUILDING BUSINESS SYSTEMS

Your guide to creating a profitable, sustainable and scaleable business

The foundation of every business are the every day tasks required to earn revenue, manage infrastructure and grow. As businesses become more successful this foundation is usually left unchanged until it starts to crack. As people settle into their roles they find better ways to do some tasks and leave others alone because "that's the way it has always been done".

Not growing your systems with your business leads to losses in potential revenue, morale and can prevent innovation and growth.

If you could take a pro-active approach to prevent these problems and save time and money without overhauling your entire infrastructure at once would you?

Would you like to implement new tools while understanding and directing their development?

Do you want to learn how to continuously improve your business with measurements to back up your work?

Building Business Systems will give you the skills to do exactly that. In this book you will learn:

  • What a business systems are and how you can use them to streamline and scale your business
  • How to use your existing policies, procedures and training to help design your system
  • How to strategically implement systems to maximize their impact without being overwhelmed by a top to bottom overhaul
  • How to reduce future training costs while simultaneously improving quality
  • How to design better tools to manage your business and your staff
  • Ways to use new tools to keep you and your staff up-to-speed on your business automatically
  • How to use data from any level of your org chart to provide value to the rest of the company
  • How to use continuous improvement to grow and improve your system with measurements to track your progress
  • How to develop culture to make your systems succeed
  • See real-world examples of how business systems have been implemented, saving companies $100,000s per year in labor costs by simplifying workflows and access to data
